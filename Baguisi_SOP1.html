Statement of the Problem

The study focuses on determining whether an innovative mop is more effective and efficient than a traditional mop in sanitation and household cleaning. It examines how well the innovative mop removes dust and dirt, how it compares in terms of cost and accessibility, and how durable and useful it is when used regularly. Overall, the research aims to find out if using an innovative mop can improve cleanliness and sanitation quality compared to a traditional mop.
